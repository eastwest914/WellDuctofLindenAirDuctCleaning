Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about air duct cleaning, dryer vent cleaning and our Linden, NJ services.

How often should I have my air ducts cleaned?

The EPA recommends cleaning air ducts every 3 to 5 years, though more frequent cleaning is advisable if you have pets, allergies, asthma, recent home renovations or visible mold or dust buildup in your vents. Our certified technicians can assess your system and recommend the best cleaning schedule for your New Jersey home or business.

Why is dryer vent cleaning so important?

Clogged dryer vents are one of the leading causes of house fires in the United States. Lint buildup restricts airflow, causes your dryer to overheat and creates a serious fire hazard. Regular dryer vent cleaning also reduces drying times, lowers energy costs and extends the lifespan of your appliance — making it one of the most important home maintenance tasks you can schedule.
